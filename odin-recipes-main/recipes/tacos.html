Tacos

Description

This recipe will allow you to make simple tacos that can feed 2-4 people. It does not take long, and there are only a handful of ingredients neccessary.

Ingredients

  • Meat (of your choice)
  • Taco seasoning mix (of your choice)
  • Torillas (hard or soft)
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by fully cooking your meat in a non-stick pan.
  2. Then, follow the instructions on your taco seasoning mix, and pour it in with the meat.
  3. Let it simmer for a few minutes and serve on a tortilla with cheese (and sour cream if you want).
